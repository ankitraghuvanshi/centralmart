Shipping FAQs Close

What do you mean by business days?

Business days are all days except Sundays and public holidays. Public holidays differ from State to State, hence a 1-2 day delay may happen if there is a public holiday in a particular state.

What do you mean by product cannot be shipped to my location?

Please enter your pin code on the product page to check if the product can be delivered to your location. Whether we can ship products to your location depends on multiple factors such as volume of the product, legal restrictions (if any), in shipping particular products to your location, availability of dependable courier partners in your location. Shipping products to particular locations is entirely our discretion.

How long will it take for me to receive the order?

The amount of time it takes for you to receive your order depends on your location. An estimate of the transit time (surface transport) is as follows:

North Zone 4-5 days

East Zone 7-8 days

West Zone 2-4 days

South Zone 5-6 days

Special Zone 12-14 days

The following is an indicative list of cities falling under each zone:

North Zone: Punjab, Chandigarh, Haryana, Delhi, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh

East Zone: Bihar, Jharkhand, Chattisgarh, West Bengal, Orissa

West Zone: Gujarat, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Goa, Daman & Diu, Dadra & Nagar Haveli

South Zone: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Kerala

Special Zone: Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Sikkim, Tripura, Manipur, Andaman & Nicobar

What is Outside Delivery Area (ODA)?

In case your location falls outside the standard delivery area of our courier partners, then there may be an outside delivery area charge (ODA) which will be applicable. In such a case, our customer service representatives will get in touch with you and additional charges, if any, would be borne by the customer.

Can I get my order faster if I am willing to pay more?

Yes, we can arrange for express shipment by air. However, there would be additional charges. After you place your order, please call our customer helpline on +91 8080 740 740.

On which days do you not deliver?

Orders are only delivered on business days. Apart from Sundays, there are certain holidays throughout the year that are not considered as business days. Deliveries will not happen on these days.
